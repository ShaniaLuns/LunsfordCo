Designs that Tell Stories. Branding that Makes an Impact.

Welcome to Lunsford & Co., My name is Shania Lunsford I'm a multidisciplinary designer specializing in branding, graphic design, illustration, and photography. I help brands, nonprofits, and communities come to life through thoughtful design that helps connect people.

Graphic Designs

My graphic design work focuses on creating clean, impactful visuals that communicate a story. From promotional materials and social media graphics to event posters and digital assets, I combine strong typography, balanced layouts, and intentional color choices to produce designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. I enjoy blending creativity with strategy to deliver designs that elevate brands and capture audiences.

Photography

My photography captures authentic moments through a creative lens, focusing on composition, mood, and storytelling. Whether I'm photographing people, landscapes, or events, I aim to preserve genuine emotion and visual harmony. My work often plays with natural lighting, depth, and subtle edits to maintain a clean and timeless aesthetic while enhancing each subject’s unique character.

Illustrations & Art Work

My illustration and logo design practice combines hand-drawn creativity with digital precision. I create visually engaging illustrations that often feature playful, whimsical, or narrative-driven elements. For logos, I focus on clean, versatile designs that reflect a brand's identity and are adaptable across platforms. My goal is always to produce thoughtful visuals that resonate with both clients and audiences.
